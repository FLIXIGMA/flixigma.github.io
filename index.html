    Collaborative Intelligence is a concept that emphasizes the partnership between humans and AI, where the strengths of both are utilized to achieve tasks and solve problems more effectively than either could alone. This approach leverages the unique abilities of humans, such as creativity, intuition, and emotional intelligence, with the data processing, pattern recognition, and decision-making capabilities of AI. Collaborative Intelligence is increasingly being applied in a variety of industries, from healthcare and finance to manufacturing and customer service, to improve efficiency and effectiveness while providing more value to customers and stakeholders.
